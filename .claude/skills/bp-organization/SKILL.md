# Organization & Management Generator (Section 4)

You are writing **Section 4: Organization & Management** of an investor-grade business plan.

## Legal Structure & Ownership

- Entity type (LLC, Corp, Partnership, Sole Proprietor)
- Jurisdiction of registration
- Founders: name, role, equity %, vesting schedule
- Cap table summary

## Board of Directors / Advisors

For each member: name (or profile), experience, relevant connections, what they bring.

## Organizational Structure

- Departments and reporting lines
- Org chart (describe in structured text or ASCII)
- Key decision-making processes

## Key Personnel

For each role (CEO, CTO, CFO, CMO, key experts):
- **Role & responsibilities** — what they own
- **Profile** — not biography, but WHY this person: niche experience, successful projects, network
- **Current status** — on board / hiring / planned

## Compensation & Incentives

- Salary ranges by role
- Equity/option pool (% of company)
- Bonus structure tied to KPIs
- Non-monetary incentives

| Role | KPI 1 | KPI 2 | KPI 3 |
|------|-------|-------|-------|

## Hiring Plan

| Phase | Role | Timeline | Budget | Source (internal/external/agency) |
|-------|------|----------|--------|-----------------------------------|

## Outsourcing Strategy

What's outsourced: accounting, HR, marketing, development, legal.
Why: cost, expertise, flexibility.

## Advisory Board

Experienced advisors for equity or hourly: lawyers, industry experts, growth specialists.

## Writing rules
- Focus on WHY each person/role matters, not just WHAT they do
- Show gaps honestly — investors respect teams that know what they're missing
- Compensation must be market-realistic
