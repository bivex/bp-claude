## Appendix: Technical Definitions

**AI Content Generation:** The use of artificial intelligence tools (including large language models, video synthesis models, and audio generation tools) to create video content without human manual editing.

**Account Warming:** The process of gradually increasing activity on a new TikTok account to build trust with the platform's algorithm and avoid flagging as suspicious or automated.

**Cluster Strategy:** Organizing accounts into groups of 25 accounts focused on specific niches (e.g., fitness, finance, beauty) to maximize relevance to target audiences while minimizing cross-contamination.

**CPV (Cost Per View):** The total cost to generate 1,000 views on TikTok content, including content production, infrastructure, and account management costs.

**CTR (Click-Through Rate):** The percentage of viewers who click from TikTok content to the destination resource (bio link, in-video CTA).

**Account Churn:** The rate at which accounts are banned, suspended, or become inactive, requiring replacement with new accounts.
