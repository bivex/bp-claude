## 1.2 Product / Service

We operate a distributed network of 150 TikTok accounts that algorithmically generate and distribute AI-created content at scale. Each account functions as an autonomous content node, producing niche-specific videos that drive targeted traffic to monetized destination resources (affiliate offers, owned products, lead generation funnels, or sponsored landing pages).

**Core Product Components:**

1. **AI Content Pipeline:** Automated video generation using AI tools—combining trending audio, viral formats, and niche-specific hooks to produce 500+ videos daily at <$0.10 per video

2. **Multi-Account Management System:** Centralized dashboard coordinating content publishing across 150 accounts, each positioned in specific sub-niches to maximize organic reach while maintaining platform compliance

3. **Traffic Funnel Optimization:** Content directs viewers to optimized destination resources through bio links, in-video calls-to-action, and comment automation, converting views into revenue (sales, leads, or ad revenue)

4. **Account Lifecycle Management:** Proprietary system handling account creation, warming (30-60 day onboarding), content scheduling, performance monitoring, and rotation to mitigate platform ban risks

5. **B2B Service Offering:** Three-tier subscription model ($2.5K-$20K/month) delivering guaranteed view volume to business clients with full-service campaign management and analytics

**Current Operational Status:**
- 150 accounts LIVE and publishing content
- 500+ videos generated and distributed daily
- 15M+ monthly views generated
- AI content pipeline operational at scale
- Account warming and rotation protocols proven
