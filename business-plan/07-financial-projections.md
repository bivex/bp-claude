# 7. Financial Projections

## 7.1 Executive Summary of Financials

Our financial model projects a path to $1.37M-$5.48M ARR within 36 months, driven by two revenue streams: B2B client subscriptions and direct monetization (affiliate, lead generation, and TikTok Creator Rewards). The business achieves positive unit economics immediately, with gross margins of 55-75% on B2B revenue and break-even operations projected by Month 14-18 depending on scenario.

**Key Financial Highlights:**
- **Year 1 Revenue:** $500K-750K (conservative base case)
- **Year 2 Revenue:** $1.5M-2.25M (scaling B2B operations)
- **Year 3 Revenue:** $3M-5M (mature operations with platform diversification)
- **Gross Margins:** 55-75% (B2B), 40-60% (direct monetization)
- **Break-Even:** Month 14 (conservative), Month 10 (moderate), Month 7 (optimistic)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** 27:1 (conservative) to 137:1 (optimistic), with 63:1 base case
- **Payback Period:** <1 month for customer acquisition

The business requires $500K-750K in seed funding to reach positive cash flow, with capital deployed primarily across team expansion ($350K-400K), infrastructure scaling ($100K-150K), and marketing/sales initiatives ($75K-125K).

**Critical Financial Assumptions:**
- [ASSUMPTION] TikTok platform remains operational and accessible in target markets
- [ASSUMPTION] Account churn rate stabilizes at 5-10% monthly with proper warming protocols
- [ASSUMPTION] Average RPM of $0.50-2.00 across monetization channels
- [ASSUMPTION] B2B client acquisition costs remain below $1,500 per client
- [ASSUMPTION] No significant TikTok ToS violations resulting in mass account bans
