## 11.5 Red Flag Preparation

**Anticipated Investor Questions / Concerns**:

1. **Platform Policy Risk**
   - **Question**: "How do you know this complies with TikTok ToS?"
   - **Preparation**: Legal opinion letter from platform policy expert, compliance documentation, ToS analysis

2. **Account Bans**
   - **Question**: "What happens when accounts get banned?"
   - **Preparation**: Historical ban rate data, recovery procedures, buffer account strategy

3. **AI Content Copyright**
   - **Question**: "Who owns the AI-generated content?"
   - **Preparation**: IP analysis, copyright counsel opinion, content licensing documentation

4. **Scalability**
   - **Question**: "Can you really manage 150+ accounts?"
   - **Preparation**: Automation architecture, operational processes, account management case studies

5. **Competition**
   - **Question**: "What stops competitors from copying this?"
   - **Preparation**: Technology differentiation, first-mover advantage, data moat analysis

6. **Monetization Sustainability**
   - **Question**: "How do you maintain monetization rates?"
   - **Preparation**: Partner relationships, conversion optimization, diversification strategy

7. **Regulatory Risk**
   - **Question**: "What about AI content regulations?"
   - **Preparation**: Regulatory monitoring, disclosure readiness, adaptability infrastructure
