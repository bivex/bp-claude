## 4.1 Legal Structure & Ownership

### Entity Type & Jurisdiction

**Recommended Structure**: Delaware C-Corporation

**Rationale**:
- [ASSUMPTION] Seeking venture capital funding — C-Corp is standard requirement for VCs
- Delaware provides established case law for corporate governance matters
- Favorable tax treatment for stock options and equity grants
- Clear path to future IPO or acquisition exit

**Registration**: Delaware Secretary of State, Foreign qualification in [FOUNDING STATE] for operations

**Alternative Considered**: LLC — Rejected due to VC preference for C-Corp structure and potential self-employment tax complications at scale

### Vesting & Anti-Dilution Provisions

- **Standard vesting**: 4 years with 1-year cliff for all founders
- **Acceleration**: Single-trigger acceleration on change of control (sale of company)
- **Repurchase rights**: Company retains right to repurchase unvested shares at cost upon departure
- **ROFR**: Right of First Refusal on any founder share transfers

## 4.9 Company Culture & Values

Given the unique nature of this business (AI-generated content, platform dependency, scaling operations), our culture emphasizes:

1. **Platform Compliance First** — Every decision considers platform policy implications
2. **Data-Driven Experimentation** — Test everything, scale what works
3. **Resilience & Adaptability** — Platforms change; we adapt faster
4. **Transparent Communication** — Account issues, policy changes, risks shared openly
5. **Sustainable Growth** — Long-term viability over short-term hacks

**Remote-First Culture**: Distributed team with async communication tools (Slack, Notion, Loom)

**Quarterly Team Retreats**: In-person collaboration and culture building post-Series A

## 4.11 Organizational Risks

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Key person dependency on founder | Documentation of processes, advisor engagement, succession planning |
| Scaling operations across 150+ accounts | Investment in automation, clear SOPs, tiered account management |
| Platform policy knowledge gaps | Advisory board engagement, ongoing training, legal counsel retention |
| Hiring competition for AI/ML talent | Competitive equity packages, remote flexibility, interesting technical challenges |
| Culture dilution with rapid growth | Values articulation, regular communication, intentional onboarding |
